Idea Incubatory
This is a webpage where I put ideas I really want to put into fruition in the future, so I claim them before others do.
  • Sonic Chaos Coding (c. April 2024): An educational Sonic the Hedgehog fangame that seeks to teach programming and computer science concepts to children ages 8-12, all with the familarity of Sonic's world.
  • Sonic of the Rogues (c. February 2024): A Sonic the Hedgehog fan board game that takes place in an alternate universe where your favorite Sonic heroes are Babylon Rogue-esque thieves, alongside the actual Babylon Rogues. Gameplay may involve a combination of racing and looting.
  • Plantaeford (c. February 2024): A (trading?) card game where you play as anthropomorphic plants who keep all sorts of animals as pets and try to outperform each other in adventures in the eponymous town of Planteford.
