Characters

Sonic & co

Sonic and his best friends! No matter how far they may live from each other, they'll always stick together to fight the forces of evil on the battlefield, as well as hang out with each other and eat snacks around the television.

Allies

While they are not closely tied with Sonic, they're always on his side and will be eager to help him.

Knights of Acorn

These non-conventional knights were appointed by King Negal Acorn to defend the Kingdom of Acorn. Amusingly, the Knights' leader is the Princess.

Spiral Hill inhabitants

They hail from and defend this town in northern Pacificscresent and its surroundings.

Villains

The rotten ones who made the voluntary choice to be and stay evil, how dare them! Thus, the heroes, Sonic, company, and his allies try to defeat them.

Eggman Empire

Doctor Eggman seeks world domination with his totaliarian empire.

Other

They are neutral towards Sonic and co, so they are categorized as "Other" here.

Babylon Rogues

This group may be (now) small in size, but they're the most notorious thieves on Mobius! The group has lasted since the ancient times and its members are descended from extinct bird-like, genie-like aliens known as the Babylonians. Besides thieving, they happen to be the most skilled users of Extreme Gear, which the Babylonians invented, on Mobius. The current generation are rivals with Team Sonic, by the way.
